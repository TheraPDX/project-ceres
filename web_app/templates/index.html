Introducing Project Ceres

Project Ceres makes it possible to monitor, control and retrieve data from your indoor garden. A special focus is given to the design of the connected device to be non-intrusive and modular.

Step 1 : Drafts

CAD drafts of the device

The Ceres shelf consists of an 2cm oak plank. The tank is integrated discreetly in the board. The opening is closed by a plexigas board which protects the electronics.

Ceres control schema of lights

The led ring informs the user of ongoing actions, like watering or data sending.
